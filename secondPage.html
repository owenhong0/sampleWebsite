Thai Red Duck Curry, known as Kaeng Phet Ped in Thai, is a rich and flavorful dish that combines tender duck meat with a fragrant, spicy, and aromatic curry sauce. This popular Thai dish features a unique blend of ingredients that highlight the balance of sweet, savory, and spicy flavors characteristic of Thai cuisine. The curry is made with a base of red curry paste, which is typically composed of red chilies, garlic, lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, galangal, and shrimp paste, giving it a vibrant, fiery kick. Coconut milk is added to create a creamy and slightly sweet consistency that perfectly complements the heat of the curry paste. The duck, often marinated with spices, is cooked until tender, absorbing the curry's rich flavors. Thai Red Duck Curry is often garnished with fresh herbs like Thai basil, cilantro, and additional kaffir lime leaves. The dish is served with steamed jasmine rice, which helps balance the heat while soaking up the flavorful curry sauce. This dish is a beloved part of Thai cuisine, offering a luxurious combination of spicy, sweet, and savory elements.
